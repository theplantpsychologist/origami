Street Fighter

Designed and folded July 2020

Paper: 40cm double tissue

The main thing that makes this design cool are the color changes. It really looks like he's wearing clothes, even though they're just flaps protruding from his legs and shoulders.


Two hints if you plan to fold this model. First, he does not have four arms, the flaps in the top corner get color changed and go over his shoulders to form the vest. Second, don't worry or precrease too much in the headband.

Grid: 42x42
