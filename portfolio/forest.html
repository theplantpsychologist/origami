Forest path

Designed and folded August 2020

Paper: 60cm double tissue

This was another one of those things that I designed just to see how complex I could go. This was also right after folding the ryujin 3.5, and scales were still fresh in my head--so naturally, when I returned to the problem of creating multiple trees from the same sheet, the solution that I thought of for creating leafy textures was something similar to the scales used in models like ryujin.


Each corner of the cp is one of the 4 trees, with two small trees on top and two big trees at the bottom. There's quite a lot of wasted space, but it gets the job done and creates indeed a forest from a single sheet of paper.

Grid: 56x56, with partial 112ths for the leaf pleats.
