Cowboy

Designed and folded July 2022

A tricky multi-subject model, and the first real model I designed and folded while at MIT.

One challenge presented by this model is that there is quite a large difference in the proportions of the horse and the rider. This meant that I had to not only figure out the correct proportions of a horse, but also make it fit the proportions of the rider. I also had to figure out how to change the grid size of a very large section of the paper for the rider.


Grid size: 32 with partial 64ths

The other main challenge was figuring out the connection between the horse's back and the guy's legs--having a river protrude from the middle of a closed back is quite difficult. It's a bit hard to describe what I did but if you look closely at the cp you might see what my jank solution was. After all, efficient designing is all about pulling off illusions, isn't it.
