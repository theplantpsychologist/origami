Penguin

Designed and folded October 2020

Paper: 25cm wenzhou tissue

Probably the best thing about this model is how convenient it is; you can easily fold it from a normal 15cm square of kami, and it has a very high impressiveness to effort ratio.

I ended up teaching this model at a Bolivian online convention, in Spanish. To practice for that, I recorded this tutorial and sent it to the organizers to make sure I was using the right words and stuff.


Make sure to avoid unecessary creases, especially for a model like this with big wide surfaces (face and belly) where extra creases are very obvious and unpleasant.

hint hint: are you looking to impress a certain someone with something round and cute but all you have is a 15cm square of duo kami? this is a good option, just sayin
