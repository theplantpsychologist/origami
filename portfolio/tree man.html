Tree man

Designed and folded November 2018

Paper: 45cm alios kraft

An experiment in creating texture and faces.


The cp is symmetrical, so I only drew half of it because back in the day I didn't know how to use the mirror tool in oripa. The face is quite unique here: the eyes are created by the "holes" that form from inverted center flaps, and right below them is a normal center flap for the nose. The woody texture is created by randomly shifting up and down the long horizontal pleats, drawn as the greyed out diagonal lines on the cp

Grid: 32x32 with partial 64ths
