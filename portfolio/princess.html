Princess

Designed and folded July 2019

Paper: 40cm tracing paper

This was the first human figure that I would say I really got right. The shaping was just ok (partly because this particular tracing paper was not great), but the proportions and the hair used in this design have worked pretty well since for other models since this one.


Not a completely correct cp, but it's what I drew at the time.
