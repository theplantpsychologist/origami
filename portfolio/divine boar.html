Divine Boar

Designed and folded February 2021

Paper: 35cm wenzhou

A tilted grid translation of Satoshi Kamiya's divine boar, which was originally based on this Studio Ghibli character. The proportions really are quite strange on this one, especially the size of the head compared to the legs, but since it's a fictional animal we get to do whatever we want.
