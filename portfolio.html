Origami Designs

A collection of the best original models I've designed over the years, along with their crease patterns. I've also included throughout the portfolio some old designs that weren't great at first but at the time were my best work. I hope that they may inspire aspiring origami designers to see how much you can improve in just a few years.

Click on a model to see the cp and read the story behind the design. Everything you see here is folded from a single uncut square of paper.
