The bottom shelf

A collection of original designs that I do like, but not enough to put it in the main portfolio; thus, if this were a physical display, these would be put on the bottom shelf.

There are many more designs beyond what is on this page and the portfolio--but most of them aren't much worth seeing. In the past, other origami artists and I have participated in Inktober (a new design every day for a month) or speed designing challenges (design something within 10 minutes), and only the few of the outcomes of such activities has actually led to interesting designs. But here are some of them.
